VIDIUS and the HKU Student Union are jointly organizing an afternoon to make signs and banners on Thursday, October 16th. This will take place in the Atrium of HKU Oudenoord at 5 PM. We hope to see you on both days! Bring fabric, cardboard, paint, and sewing supplies!
We might go for a drink in a park afterwards, or we'll keep chilling in the oudenoord atrium! We will supply our ants with many many snacks and drinks and maybe even a beer. Come have fun and get to meet some of your frants.
